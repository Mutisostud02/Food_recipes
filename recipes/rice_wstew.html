Rice With Stew

Description

We are going to prepare meat stew to be served with rice.

Have utensils ready,cooking dish,knives,cooking spoons and the cooker ready.

Ingredients

  • Cooking fat
  • Salt
  • Royco spice
  • boiled water
  • Rice
  • Dania leaves
  • Tomato paste
  • Onions
  • Carrots
  • Meat pieces
  • Chilli sauce

Steps

  1. Fry Onions in cooking fat till it turns brown and add water into the cooking dish.
  2. Boil water and add Salt.
  3. Add rice in the boiled water,cover the dish and boil it with low heat for 15 minutes.
  4. Remove the rice and boil the meat pieces on another cooking dish.
  5. Boil till the water steams out.
  6. Add onions and cooking fat into the meat and fry them till brown.
  7. Add tomato paste and cook for 3 minutes then add carrots.
  8. Cook the food and add Royco spices and salt.
  9. Cook for 3 minutes and add dania leaves and cook for a while.
  10. Add water just below meat level in the cooking dish and boil it.
  11. Put some chilli sauce and cover the food.
  12. Switch off the heater.
  13. Food is ready
