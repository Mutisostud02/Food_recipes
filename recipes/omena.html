Omena Recipe

Description

This is the preparation of omena, a small species of food with a sweet spicy taste.

Ingredients

  • Omena
  • Royco spice
  • Dania leaves
  • Tomato paste
  • 2 Onions
  • Cooking fat

Steps

  1. Wash the omena/dagaa in some warm water.
  2. Dry the omena in a sufuria and save it in a plate.
  3. Fry the omena in a cooking dish with some cooking fat.
  4. Fry till its brown and crunchy and add onions.
  5. Fry it for two minutes and add tomato paste.
  6. Cook for 5 minutes and add salt and Royco spice.
  7. Add some dania leaves and cook for 2 minutes.
